Internet

    Internet is one of the latest wireless and telecommunication media. It means international Networking system through cyber process. It works with the help of Computer and networks. It is practically a network of communication. In Bangladesh, Internet has opened a new dimension in our communication system. The use of internet in our country still remains costly. Modern age is the age of information. Information is stored through internet. Now the world commerce has been changed by it. Internet based commerce has become most popular with the customers. They can buy or choose anything without going to market. Educational institutions are also enjoying its various advantages. In fact, it is a wonderful system in a very short time.

FaceBook

    Facebook is a familiar word now a days. It is an internet based network which connects people world wide. Anyone who has internet connection may have the access to it. Its main function is to maintain social and friendly relationship between people living in the remote corner of the world. A person must have an internet connection to be a facebook member. He must have a secret password to ensure privacy and security. A man can search his friends and near ones through facebook he can send invitation. He can write comment on his status. He may give his own pictures. It has become a game to the young people. It has some bad sides also. Sometimes the boys or girls upload obscene and objectionable photos and comments. We should take the good ones and avoid the bad ones.

E-Mail

    We live in the age of science and technology. E-mail is one of the advantages of science. It benefits us in the world of communication. Electronic mail is popularly known as e-mail. It is the communication of textual message via electronic means. In this system a personal computer, a modem and a telephone connection are necessary. It requires e-mail account number. Messages can be transmitted from one country to another within seconds. It is far cheaper than telephone calls. Privacy is ensured. It is delivered to an individual's mail box.
Now a days trade and commerce have become greatly dependent on this speedy mode of communication. It helps us to keep our communication to any part of the world. It also maintains world wide information network with remote areas of the world.

Price hike

    As a country Bangladesh is beset with many problems. Price hike is one of them. It has become one of the great problems of our country now. The prices of essential commodities have gone beyond the control of the common people. The prices are not stable. They are increasing by leaps and bounds. The prices of rice, oil, fish, vegetables, baby's milk etc have gone beyond the control of the common people. Now the general people are facing the threat of price hike. Some reasons are responsible for this, Some dishonest businessmen are boarding the daily necessaries. The smugglers and the black marketers are also responsible for this. Some steps need to be taken to keep the price hike inder control. The smugglers must be punished. A bazar monitoring team should be employed in each area.

Sentence এর গঠনঃ

Assertive Sentence

* Subject + verb + object.

যেমনঃ I eat rice.
         He is a good boy.

Interrogative Sentence

* Auxiliary Verb + Subject + verb + object + ?

যেমনঃ Is he eating rice ?
         Do you go home ?

Imperative Sentence

Imperative Sentence এর Subject হিসাবে 'you' উহ্য থাকে ।

* মূল verb + object.

যেমনঃ Go home.
         Help the poor.

* Do not + মূল verb + object.

যেমনঃ Do not run in the sun.
         Do not make a noise.
         Do not go out.

* Let + object + Verb + object.

যেমনঃ Let me do it.
         Let him go home.

Optative Sentence

* May + Subject + verb + object.

যেমনঃ May you be happy.
         May Allah bless you.

Exclamatory Sentence

* What/How + object + Subject + verb + !

যেমনঃ How nice the flower is !
         What a big river !

* If/Had/Would that/oh that + Subject + verb + object + !

যেমনঃ If were a king !
         Had I the wings of a bird !

* Hurrah ! / Alas ! + Subject + verb + object.

যেমনঃ Hurrah! We have won the game.
         Alas! he is dead.
